Activites at Geneva-on-the-Lake

Geneva-on-the-Lake is a beautiful resort town on the shores of Lake Erie. Here are some activities you can enjoy during your stay:

At the Resort

The Lodge at Geneva-on-the-Lake offers a variety of amenities for guests to enjoy:

  • Indoor and outdoor pools, including hot tubs
  • Bicycles along the scenic Lake Erie shoreline into Geneva State Park.
  • Yard games like bocce ball, shuffleboard, and giant chess

The Strip

Geneva-on-the-Lake is known for its lively strip, which features arcades, restaurants, and shops. Here are some highlights:

  • Eddie's Grill: Famous for their foot-long hot dogs
  • Adventure Zone: Go-karts, mini-golf, bumper boats, and more
  • Old Firehouse Winery: Live music, food, and wine tastings. With a ferris wheel!
  • The Cove Niteclub: A popular bar with live music and lake views

In the Area

Free

Covered Bridges

Ashtabula County is home to 19 covered bridges, including the longest and shortest covered bridges in the United States. Take a scenic drive to see them all!

Beach

Relax on the sandy shores of Lake Erie or take a refreshing swim at the public beach in Geneva State Park.

$$

Golf

Hit the greens at one of the nearby golf courses. Geneva-on-the-Lake is home to several courses that cater to players of all skill levels.

$$$

Wine Country

Ashtabula County is home to over 20 wineries, many of which are located near Geneva-on-the-Lake. Take a wine tour and enjoy the beautiful countryside!

The Lodge at Geneva-on-the-Lake offers a Wine Shuttle that will take you to local wineries and back to the resort.

Ziplining / Adventure Course

At the Lodge, Lake Erie Canopy Tours offers ziplining and an aerial adventure course for those seeking an adrenaline rush. Experience the thrill of flying through the treetops!
